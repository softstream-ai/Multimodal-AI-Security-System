Revolutionizing security

AI-driven solutions for safety

At AiSecurity.SoftStream.tech, we are committed to enhancing safetywith our Multimodal AI-Powered Security System. This innovative solution employs advanced drone detection and intruder recognition technology to provide unparalleled protection for both residential and commercial properties. By harnessing cutting-edge AI algorithms like YOLO and FaceNet, our system monitors your environment in real time, distinguishing between known individuals and potential threats. Instant alerts via SMS or email ensure that you stay informed, empowering you with peace of mind in an ever-evolving security landscape.

Why Our System Stands Out

Advance Drone Detection

Our Advanced Drone Detection technology empowers your security system to identify unauthorized drones in real time. Utilizing YOLO, a powerful AI algorithm, the system analyzes aerial activity around your property. When a suspicious drone is detected, it immediately activates alerts, ensuring you are always aware of potential threats. This proactive approach means you can act quickly to safeguard your space. Whether it's at home or in a commercial setting, this feature enhances your security, providing the assurance that your environment remains protected from aerial intrusions.

Intruder recognition system

Facial recognition and anomaly detection for intruder identificationThe Intruder Recognition System harnesses the power of FaceNet technology to provide reliable identification of individuals within your environment. By comparing faces against a pre-existing family database, it distinguishes between familiar faces and potential intruders. This capability not only enhances security but also builds a sense of familiarity and comfort for residents. In case an unknown individual is detected, the system promptly sends alerts via SMS or email, ensuring you are always informed. This feature significantly boosts your security measures, making it an essential part of your safety strategy.

Real-Time Alerts and notificaion

Our Real-Time Alerts and Notifications feature keeps you informed of any potential security threats as they occur. The system is designed to instantly send alerts via SMS or email, ensuring you never miss critical updates. Whether it’s an unidentified drone hovering above or an unknown individual approaching your property, you will receive immediate notifications to take appropriate action. This timely communication enhances your ability to respond to threats effectively, providing greater peace of mind. With this feature, you can rest easy knowing that your security system is always working for you.
